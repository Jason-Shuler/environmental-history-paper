# Not by Choice: Corporate Influence, the 1956 Highway Act, and the Making of Car-Centric America

**Author:** Jason Shuler  
**Year:** 2025  

This repository hosts an independent research paper based on original primary-source
analysis in American environmental history.

## Overview
This paper examines how corporate influence, propaganda, and federal policy culminated
in the Federal-Aid Highway Act of 1956, creating a car-centric and fossil fuel–dependent
transportation system in the United States.

The analysis draws on:
- archival government documents
- corporate promotional materials
- contemporary journalism
- historical scholarship
